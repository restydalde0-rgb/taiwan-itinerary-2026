Day by Day

Four Days, Zero Backtracking

Each day is built around one part of the city so you never waste time criss-crossing town. Mornings for outdoor sights (August heat is real), air-conditioned culture for the afternoon lull, and night markets after dark. Tap any day for the full hour-by-hour timeline.

Day 01 · Tuesday, 18 August

Landing Softly: Ximending, Memorials & Raohe Lights

A smart 2 AM arrival plan, a slow brunch morning at Fuhang Dou Jiang, then Taipei's classics — Chiang Kai-shek Memorial, golden hour at Taipei 101, and dinner under the lanterns of Raohe Street Night Market.

Day 02 · Wednesday, 19 August

The Classics: Elephant Mountain to Ningxia Nights

Taipei's postcard day — the skyline from Elephant Mountain's boulders, Din Tai Fung's Michelin xiao long bao, the 101 Observatory (if it's clear), Huashan's creative village, and Ningxia Night Market.

⛰️ Sunrise-ish hike🏙️ 101 Observatory🎨 Huashan 1914🍢 Ningxia Night Market

Day 03 · Thursday, 20 August

Spirited Away: Shifen Lanterns & Jiufen Tea Houses

The unmissable day trip — release a sky lantern on Shifen's live railway tracks, walk to Taiwan's broadest waterfall, then graze Jiufen Old Street and sip gongfu tea at A-Mei Tea House as the red lanterns switch on.

Day 04 · Friday, 21 August

One Last Breakfast, Then Home

A 10:20 AM international departure means a disciplined morning — wake at 6, Airport MRT Express at 7, checked in by 8. Plus a fallback plan if you oversleep, and where to grab last-minute pineapple cakes.

🛫 TPE 10:20 AM🚄 Airport MRT Express🥐 Breakfast plan
Open Day 4
🧭
The routing logic: Day 1 stays near your Ximending base and ends east (Raohe). Day 2 starts east early (beat the heat on Elephant Mountain) and spirals back west. Day 3 leaves the city entirely — Shifen first because the waterfall closes ~5 PM, Jiufen second because its lanterns peak after 5:30 PM. Day 4 is pure logistics. No route is ever walked twice.
🌦️
August weather rhythm: outdoor sights before noon, malls/cafés/museums in the 2–4 PM thunderstorm window, night markets after dark. Every day below follows this pattern — see the Essentials page for the full weather brief.
