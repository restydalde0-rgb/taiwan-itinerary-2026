🌦️ Taipei in August

Daily high / low33°C / 27°C

Strategy: outdoor sights before noon, malls/cafés/museums 2–4 PM, night markets after dark. Carry a folding umbrella always. Check the CWA app for typhoon bulletins the week before flying.

📱 Apps to Install

  • 🗺️Google Maps — transit directions are flawless in Taipei
  • 🚇Taipei Metro Go — official MRT app, fares & timetables
  • 🚌BusTracker Taipei (Bus+) — live bus arrivals, crucial for Jiufen day
  • 🎫Klook / KKday — discounted 101 tickets, eSIMs, day tours
  • 🈶Google Translate — download offline Chinese (Traditional) + camera mode
  • 🌀CWA Weather — official typhoon warnings

📶 SIM & Connectivity

  • 💾eSIM (recommended): buy on Klook/KKday before flying — cheaper than airport counters and active the moment you land at 2 AM
  • 📇Physical SIM: Chunghwa, FarEasTone and T Star counters at Taoyuan arrivals sell unlimited-data tourist SIMs (3–5 days, roughly ) — some open around the clock, but confirm for a 2 AM landing
  • 🛰️Coverage: excellent everywhere on this itinerary, including Jiufen and the Pingxi valley
  • 📡Free Wi-Fi: MRT stations and convenience stores have it, but don't rely on it for navigation

🔌 Power & Plugs

Plug typesA & B (same as PH)
Voltage110V · 60Hz
Adapter needed?No — PH devices fit
USB chargingMRT Express & most cafés

Taiwan uses the same flat-pin plugs as the Philippines at 110V — everything modern (phones, cameras, power banks) charges fine. Pack one power bank per person for the long photo days; it's the single most-used item on this list.

📌 Travel Reminders

  • 🛂Verify Taiwan's current entry rules for Philippine passport holders before flying (visa-free windows get extended/changed)
  • 🏠Message your host about the 2:00 AM arrival the day you book — confirm anytime self check-in
  • 🌀Watch CWA typhoon bulletins from ~Aug 11; know your airline's rebooking policy
  • 💵Night markets and old streets are cash-first — keep small bills
  • 🚇No eating or drinking past MRT fare gates (fines are real!)
  • 📸Screenshot your stay's address in Chinese for taxi drivers
