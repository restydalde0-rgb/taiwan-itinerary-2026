Where to Stay

5 Stays, One Winner

All five are inside the Ximending / Taipei Main Station / Zhongshan golden triangle — the best base for first-timers because the Airport MRT, three metro lines and two food streets converge here. Prices are typical nightly rates for 2 adults in mid-August; always re-check live availability for Aug 18–21, 2026 before booking.

🏆 Best Overall
🏙️
Ximending / Taipei Main

XN734 · Taipei Main Station Apartment

Modern private apartment in Wanhua District, sitting exactly between Taipei Main Station and Ximending.

≈ NT$2,600/ night · ≈ NT$7,800 (₱15,050) for 3 nights
  • 🚇6–8 min walk to Ximen or Taipei Main Station MRT
  • 📍Nearby: Ximending walking streets, Red House, North Gate, Taipei Main food halls
  • 🛋️Amenities: AC, Wi-Fi, washer, kitchenette, self check-in lockbox
Pros
  • Unbeatable dual location
  • Self check-in (great for 2 AM plans)
  • Direct Airport MRT access
Cons
  • Older building exterior
  • Street noise on weekends
💡 Why it wins: Everything in this itinerary starts or ends at Ximen or Taipei Main. Self check-in solves the 2 AM arrival, and Day 4's airport run becomes a 40-minute door-to-terminal trip.

Zhongzheng · near Ximending

Taipei Station Group Apartment

Spacious multi-bed apartment in Zhongzheng District — more room than a couple needs, at a shared-cost sweet spot.

≈ NT$2,900/ night · ≈ NT$8,700 (₱16,790) for 3 nights
  • 🚇~7 min walk to Taipei Main Station
  • 📍Nearby: 228 Peace Park, Ximending, Q Square Mall
  • 🛋️Amenities: AC, Wi-Fi, multiple beds, dining area
Pros
  • Lots of space
  • Steps from Airport MRT terminus
Cons
  • Sized for groups, not couples
  • Basic decor
💡 Why recommended: Best pick if friends join later or you want maximum space per peso near the station.

Taipei Main Station

Star Hostel Taipei Main — Private Double

Taipei's most-loved designer hostel: a plant-filled loft lounge, famous free breakfast, and hotel-clean private rooms.

≈ NT$2,300/ night · ≈ NT$6,900 (₱13,320) for 3 nights
  • 🚇5 min walk to Taipei Main Station (Y-district exits)
  • 📍Nearby: Airport MRT, Q Square, Huayin Street eats
  • 🛋️Amenities: free breakfast, 24-hr reception, luggage storage, lounge
Pros
  • 24-hr front desk — perfect for 2 AM arrival
  • Free breakfast & luggage storage
Cons
  • Compact rooms
  • Hostel vibe isn't for everyone
💡 Why recommended: The safest logistical choice — a staffed desk at 3:30 AM beats any lockbox if you value zero friction.

Ximending core

Meander 1948 — Private Double

A restored 1948 building in the middle of Ximending's neon — boutique-hostel private rooms right on the walking streets.

≈ NT$2,700/ night · ≈ NT$8,100 (₱15,630) for 3 nights
  • 🚇3 min walk to Ximen MRT (Blue & Green lines)
  • 📍Nearby: Red House, Ximending street food, cinema street murals
  • 🛋️Amenities: 24-hr desk, rooftop, laundry, luggage room
Pros
  • You live inside the night-life district
  • Photogenic heritage interiors
Cons
  • Weekend crowd noise
  • 1 transfer to Airport MRT
💡 Why recommended: If atmosphere beats convenience for you, waking up inside Ximending is the most "Taipei" experience on this list.

Zhongshan

Zhongshan Designer Studio (Airbnb area pick)

Zhongshan is Taipei's café-and-boutique quarter — pick any highly-rated studio within 5 minutes of Zhongshan MRT.

≈ NT$3,000/ night · ≈ NT$9,000 (₱17,370) for 3 nights
  • 🚇~5 min walk to Zhongshan MRT (Red & Green lines)
  • 📍Nearby: Chifeng Street cafés, Ningxia Night Market (12 min walk), Dadaocheng
  • 🛋️Amenities: typically AC, Wi-Fi, kitchenette, washer
Pros
  • Best café density in Taipei
  • Walk home from Ningxia Night Market
Cons
  • Priciest of the five
  • One stop removed from Ximending
💡 Why recommended: Tailor-made for the café-loving half of this trip — browse live listings in the linked search.
