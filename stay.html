Pros
  • Only location where all four days start with zero or one transit leg
  • Self check-in solves the 2 AM arrival without a night-desk surcharge

  • Ximending's food, shopping and nightlife are your literal doorstep

Cons
  • Older building exterior — don't judge by the lobby
