Getting Around

Transport, Decoded

Taipei has one of Asia's cleanest, easiest transit systems. One card runs everything — here's every mode you'll touch on this trip, with fares.

💳

EasyCard (悠遊卡)

  • Buy at any airport/MRT convenience store: NT$100 for the card + top-up (start with NT$500 each).
  • Taps onto MRT, buses, TRA trains (incl. Ruifang!), YouBike and convenience stores.
  • 20% off MRT fares vs single tickets + free bus↔MRT transfer discounts.
  • Top up in cash at any 7-Eleven or station machine.
🚄

Airport MRT

  • Express train: Taipei Main ↔ terminals in 36–39 min, NT$150.
  • Runs ~6:00 AM – 11:00 PM+ (not overnight — hence the Day 1 night-bus plan).
  • Purple line; Express has plush seats & luggage racks.
  • In-town check-in for select airlines at A1 Taipei Main.
🚇

Taipei MRT

  • Fares NT$20–65 by distance; this itinerary averages NT$25/ride.
  • Lines are color-coded — you'll mostly use Blue (Bannan), Green (Songshan-Xindian) and Red (Tamsui-Xinyi).
  • Runs 6:00 AM – midnight, trains every 3–7 min.
  • No eating or drinking past the fare gates (fines are real!).
🚌

Buses & Day-trip Rail

  • City buses NT$15/section, tap on and off with EasyCard.
  • Bus 1819: 24-hr airport ↔ Taipei Main, NT$140.
  • Day 3: TRA to Ruifang NT$49–76 · Pingxi Line NT$20 · bus to Jiufen NT$15 · bus 1062 home NT$102.
  • Google Maps transit directions are excellent in Taipei.
🚕

Taxis & Ride-hailing

  • Yellow taxis are metered, honest and everywhere; flag-down starts around NT$85, most cross-town hops land at NT$150–250.
  • Uber works in Taipei and shows the fare upfront — handy when you're tired of decoding bus stops.
  • Airport runs: Ximending ↔ TPE is 40–50 min, NT$1,100–1,300 — the Day 4 emergency fallback.
  • Show addresses in Chinese (screenshot your stay's listing) — most drivers speak little English.
🚲

YouBike (bonus)

  • Taipei's shared bikes, docked at nearly every MRT exit — tap the same EasyCard.
  • Cheap (NT$10/30 min in the city) and lovely for riverside parks or Zhongshan's lanes.
  • Register the card once at a kiosk or via the YouBike app.
  • Skip it in an August downpour — the MRT wins.
💰
Estimated total transport spend: ≈ NT$1,250 per person (₱2,410) for the whole trip — airport transfers NT$290, three days of MRT/bus ~NT$450, and the full Jiufen/Shifen loop ~NT$250, plus buffer. Load NT$600 onto each EasyCard on arrival and top up once mid-trip.
