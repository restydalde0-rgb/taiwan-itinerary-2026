The math behind the clock: international check-in wants you at TPE ~2.5 hrs early (7:40 AM). The Express train takes ~36 min, and the walk to the A1 platform inside Taipei Main takes 10 more. Working backwards from 7:40 gives a 6:30 departure from the apartment — with a 30-minute cushion already built in.
Hour by Hour

The Timeline

6:00 AM

⏰ Wake, final pack, check out

Pack the night before. Leave the key per host instructions and photograph the room (deposit protection).

6:30 AM 🚪 Leave apartment

🚶 Walk to Taipei Main → Airport MRT (A1)

🚄 Apartment → Taoyuan Airport
  1. 🚶6:30 — walk ~10 min to Taipei Main Station.
  2. 🧭6:40 — follow the purple Airport MRT (A1) signs — the terminal is west of the main hall; allow 10 min of indoor walking.
  3. 🛄6:50 — bonus: many airlines offer in-town check-in at A1 (bag drop up to 3 hrs before departure). If yours participates, drop bags here and fly through the airport.
  4. 🚄7:00 — board the Express train (not Commuter) — every ~15 min from ~6:00 AM. Ride 36–39 min.
  5. 🛬~7:40 — arrive at your terminal (check T1 vs T2 for your airline).
⏱️ Est. total: ~70 min door-to-terminal💰 Fare: 📏 ~40 km
🚇 Airport MRT timetable
7:45 AM

🛄 Airport check-in — 2.5 hrs before

Counters for a 10:20 flight open around 7:20–7:50. Check in, clear security and immigration by ~8:45, leaving a relaxed 60+ minutes at the gate.

8:30 AM

🥡 Breakfast options (pick one)

Fastest: a fan tuan + soy milk from the shops near Taipei Main before boarding (~). Airside: Taoyuan's food courts do decent beef noodles and dumplings post-security (~). Last pasalubong: Chia Te and SunnyHills pineapple cakes are sold in the departure halls — buy here if you skipped them in town (from the shopping allowance, ~).

💰
10:20 AM

🛫 Wheels up — TPE → MNL

Land in Manila 1:05 PM with an EasyCard souvenir, a camera roll full of lanterns, and (statistically) 1.5 kg of pineapple cake.

🚨
Fallback: if you somehow miss the ~7:15 Express, a taxi from Ximending to TPE is 40–50 min and — expensive but flight-saving. Don't cut it closer than a 7:30 AM train.
